Transport

Pendrell Hall closes at 12am (midnight) and all non-resident guests must leave by 12.30am when the gates are locked. Guests are welcome to leave their cars overnight in our discreet car park, all vehicles must be collected by 10am the following day. The venue gates will open from 7am until 10am the following day for collection of any cars which have been left overnight
